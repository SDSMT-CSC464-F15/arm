-To get internet connection on the ODroids, first plug in an ethernet cable connected to the internet.
-Then edit 
(sudo) /etc/network/interfaces 
-to add # in front of the last four lines.

-Reset eth0 by executing:
sudo ifdown eth0
sudo ifup eth0
-Alternativly, you can reboot the system.

-To reconnect to the network on the switch, remove those # that you added and do the same two commands.
